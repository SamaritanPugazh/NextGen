Quick Stir-Fry Noodles

Easy • 20 min · Serves 2
Add an image named "noodles.jpg" beside this file
Prep: 5 min
Cook: 15 min

Ingredients

  • 200 gegg noodles
  • 1 tbspvegetable oil
  • 2 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 1small onion, thinly sliced
  • 150 gmixed vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, snap peas)
  • 2 tbspsoy sauce
  • 1 tbspoyster sauce or hoisin
  • 1 tspsesame oil
  • to tastesalt & pepper
  • optionalchopped scallions & sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions until just tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic and onion; sauté 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until crisp-tender, about 3–4 minutes.
  4. Toss in cooked noodles. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir until evenly coated and heated through.
  5. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve hot topped with scallions and sesame seeds if using.

Tip: To make it vegetarian, use a vegetarian oyster sauce or extra hoisin and add firm tofu.

Notes

Swap noodles for rice noodles for a lighter texture. For more heat add chili paste or fresh chilies.

Nutrition (est.)

Per serving: ~420 kcal, 12g protein, 14g fat, 58g carbs
